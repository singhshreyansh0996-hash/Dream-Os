🌙 DreamOS Ideas & Future Features

«This document contains ideas, concepts, and experimental features for DreamOS. Items listed here are not guarantees and may change as the project evolves.»

---

Core Vision

Create an operating system that helps people achieve their goals instead of simply launching applications.

---

Goal-Based Setup

Instead of asking only for system settings during installation, DreamOS asks:

"What is your dream?"

Examples:

- Student
- Game Developer
- Software Developer
- Designer
- Artist
- Music Producer
- Business Owner
- Content Creator

DreamOS then prepares a personalized environment based on the selected goal.
